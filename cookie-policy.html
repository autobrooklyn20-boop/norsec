Cookie Policy

Last Updated: January 7, 2026

This Cookie Policy explains how norsec ("we," "us," or "our") uses cookies and similar technologies on our website. By using our website, you consent to the use of cookies as described in this policy.

1. What Are Cookies?

Cookies are small text files that are placed on your device when you visit a website. They are widely used to make websites work more efficiently, provide a better user experience, and provide information to website owners.

2. How We Use Cookies

We use cookies for the following purposes:

  • Essential Cookies: Required for the website to function properly
  • Performance Cookies: Help us understand how visitors interact with our website
  • Functionality Cookies: Remember your preferences and settings
  • Analytics Cookies: Collect information about website usage

3. Types of Cookies We Use

Cookie Type Purpose Duration
Session Cookies Maintain your session while browsing our website Session (deleted when browser closes)
Authentication Cookies Identify you when you log in to your account Up to 30 days
Preference Cookies Remember your language and display preferences Up to 1 year
Analytics Cookies Collect information about how visitors use our site Up to 2 years
Security Cookies Help prevent fraud and protect your data Session to 1 year

4. Third-Party Cookies

We may use third-party services that set their own cookies:

  • Google Analytics: Tracks website traffic and usage patterns
    (See Google's Cookie Policy)
  • Payment Processors: Stripe and PayPal may set cookies during checkout
    (See their respective privacy policies)
  • Font Providers: Google Fonts may set cookies for font delivery

5. Managing Cookies

You can control and manage cookies in several ways:

Browser Settings

Most web browsers allow you to control cookies through their settings. You can:

  • Delete all cookies stored on your device
  • Block all cookies (though this may affect website functionality)
  • Set preferences for specific websites
  • Receive notifications when cookies are set

For instructions, check your browser's help section: All About Cookies - Manage Cookies Guide

Opt-Out Tools

You can opt out of certain analytics cookies:

  • Google Analytics: Google Analytics Opt-out Browser Add-on
  • Advertising Cookies: Your Online Choices (EU)

6. Essential Cookies Notice

Please note that some cookies are essential for the website to function properly. If you disable these cookies, certain features of our website may not work correctly, including:

  • Adding products to your shopping cart
  • Processing secure checkout
  • Remembering your login status
  • Maintaining your privacy and security preferences

7. Do Not Track Signals

Some browsers have a "Do Not Track" feature that lets you tell websites you do not want to have your online activities tracked. Currently, our website does not respond to "Do Not Track" signals because there is no standard for how websites should respond to such signals.
